

# 💗 "When You Feel Weak, Come To Me" — Romantic Interactive Website

A deeply personal, emotional, and interactive website with soft pastel aesthetics designed to make someone feel loved, safe, and cared for.

---

## 🌸 Section 1 — Soft Romantic Landing Page
- Full-screen hero with soft pink/lavender gradient background
- Main heading: *"When your health feels low, my love becomes your medicine."*
- Subtext: *"You don't have to be strong with me."*
- Glowing **"Come Closer" 💗** button that smoothly scrolls to the next section
- Subtle floating hearts animation throughout

---

## 🍼 Section 2 — Baby Care Mode
- Title: *"Today, I Take Care of My Baby."*
- 5 interactive care cards with soft glow-on-hover animation:
  - 🥣 Feed You With My Hands
  - 💊 Medicine Time
  - 🧠 Head Massage
  - 🤗 Hug Therapy
  - 😴 Sleep in My Arms
- Each card shows its loving message on hover/click

## 🍦 Section 3 — Things That Make You Smile
- Title: *"Your Favourite Things"*
